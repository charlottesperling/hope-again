Donations

A donation of about one dollar a day, $30 or more a month, will help keep this ministry available to those who need our help.

Your faith and generous support continues to fulfill the needs of many in the surrounding community. Because of you more families are given food. Because of you more families are receiving clothing. Because of you many men and women are being educated at schools or trained on the job. Because of you many battered women and children have a safe haven. And more importantly, because of you more people have received salvation found in a personal relationship with Jesus Christ!

We want you to have the opportunity to participate in the ministry of Hope Again. Help us to help others by showing God's love in action. All Financial Donations assist our ministry in covering overhead costs, purchasing supplies, and assisting in expanding our services.
