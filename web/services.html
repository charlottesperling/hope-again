Services

Potential Residents
To apply for transitional housing and programming with Hope Again:

You can come to the office any morning of Monday, Tuesday, or Thursday, to meet with a case manager. At that time, the program will be explained to you in detail. No appointments are taken for this first step. First time visitors need to fill out an intake sheet at the front desk.

If our program sounds like something you want to apply for, you will be given an application to fill out.

Following the completed application, there will be an interview with our Associate Director. If the Associate Director feels you would be a fit for our program, an appointment will be made to meet with our counselor.

This interview process may take up to a few days. Our staff will fit you into our existing schedule.

Following all inteviews, a decision will be made to either offer you a spot in our program or explain why you were not accepted.

A clean drug test and a recent TB test are required for admission to our program. Continual, random drug testing will be done throughout your stay at Hope Again. As we are a clean and sober facility, failure to maintain clean drug/alcohol tests will result in immediate termination.

Hope Again is a fee based program. Financail details will be addressed at the interview level of the application process.
