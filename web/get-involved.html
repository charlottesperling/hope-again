Get Involved

Each year hundreds of volunteers donate a few hours of their time at Hope Again. Volunteers come from churches, civic groups, and corporations. Many are individuals who simply feel a strong calling to help in any way they can.

Hundreds of other individuals, groups, and organizations donate money, food, or life skills expertise (and in many cases, all three!) to help Hope Again meet the needs of the less fortunate.

Ways you can help
  • Donate Online via Acceptiva, Paypal or by mail
  • Donate - non-perishable food items, clothing and other useable household items
  • Pray for this ministry
  • Host a Food Drive
  • Contact us to receive the monthly prayer and news letters
  • Participate in annual fundraising events
  • Volunteer
  • Write notes of encouragement to the staff

Volunteer

Please contact us at (323) 661-4004 to setup a day and time to volunteer.

You must be 16 or older to volunteer at Hope Again. However individuals between the ages of 13 and 15 can volunteer with a parent or guardian.

    Volunteer Activies
  • Sorting food and stocking shelves
  • Fundraisers and Events
  • Sitting with children
  • Office and Housing Repairs/Painting
  • Automotive mechanic services
  • Become a Mentor
  • Work groups
  • Help with bulk mailing
  • Sort donated clothing

Donations

Hope Again depends on the generosity of individuals in the community, like you, to provide items for people in need and much less fortunate than most of us. Since 1992, Hope Again has helped thousands of people. More than half of those helped are children.

Group Collection

A fun way to collect needed items for Hope Again is to organize your neighbors, congregation, co-workers or classmates to do a group collection.

If you have an idea to collect an item that is not listed, let us know. We would love to work with you to make your collection efforts successful. Email donations@hopeagain.org if you need more information about spreading hope!

    You can collect:
  • Groceries
  • Clothing
  • Diapers
  • Baby care items
  • New toys
  • Gifts and Christmas stockings
  • Toiletries
  • School supplies
  • Money
  • McDonald giftcards
  • Burger King giftcards
